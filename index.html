South Korea officially the Republic of Korea (ROK), a country in eastern Asia that occupies the southern part of the Korean peninsula. Korea is well-known and unique for its pop-culture (K-dramas and K-pop) and modern technology, however there's so much more to this East Asian country than the trends it has given birth to. This website will serve not only popular and famous tourist attractions and foods, but also its traditional origins and culture that isn’t as heavily sought for. This will hopefully inform individuals who wish to gain more knowledge on the traditions and assist them with more places to visit if they wish for more alternatives other than the usual tourist attractions.
